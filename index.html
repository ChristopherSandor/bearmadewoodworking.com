BEAR MADE

BearMade Woodworking is built on craftsmanship, creativity, and care. Every project is designed and built with precision. No shortcuts, no compromises—just quality woodworking done the way it should be. At BearMade, everything is fully custom, tailored to bring your ideas to life. From initial design to the final finish, every piece is either handmade or digitally crafted and milled to perfection. Each project starts with hand-selected wood, chosen personally by Barry—better known as “Bear”—to ensure only the finest materials are used. Bear has spent more than 30 years perfecting his craft, continuously learning, improving, and building with passion. As a one-man operation, he pours his time, skill, and dedication into every project, ensuring it's as unique as the person it's made for. Based in New Jersey, Bear's work has been found homes & businesses across the state, a testament to both quality and care. With BearMade, you're not just getting a piece of woodwork—you're getting something made with heart, experience, and pride.
